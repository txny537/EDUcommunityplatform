About EduConnect

Our story, our mission, and the people behind the platform.

Our Mission

EduConnect was founded in 2024 with one goal: to make quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of background or location. We believe that knowledge should be free, open, and community-driven.

We partner with educators, industry professionals, and volunteers to create courses that are practical, up-to-date, and relevant to today's job market.
